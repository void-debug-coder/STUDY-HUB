🏗️ Module 1: Strength of Materials

1. Introduction

Rigid bodies vs deformable solids. Stability, strength, stiffness. Concept of homogeneous, isotropic material. Principle of superposition, St. Venant principle

2. Stress & Strain

Tension, compression, shear stresses. Strain, elasticity, Hooke's Law. Limit of proportionality, modulus of elasticity. Stress-strain curve with yield point, ultimate strength, rupture

3. Elastic Constants

Lateral strain, Poisson's ratio μ. Shear modulus G, bulk modulus K. Relations: E = 2G(1+μ) = 3K(1-2μ)

4. Temperature Stresses

Deformation of simple & compound bars due to temp change

5. Biaxial Stress & Mohr's Circle

Stress at a point, stress on inclined plane, principal stresses & principal planes

6. Equilibrium of Forces

ΣF_x = 0, ΣF_y = 0, ΣM = 0. Free Body Diagrams isolate the body and show all external forces + reactions

7. Support Reactions

Roller: 1 reaction ⊥ surface. Pin: 2 reactions. Fixed: 3 reactions. Solve using equilibrium equations

📐 Key Formulas

σ = F/A → Normal Stress (MPa)
ε = ΔL/L → Normal Strain
E = σ/ε → Young's Modulus
δ = FL/AE → Axial Deformation
ΣF=0, ΣM=0 → Equilibrium
E = 2G(1+μ) = 3K(1-2μ) → Elastic Constants
